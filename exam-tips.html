1. Exam Structure
  • Total number of questions: 65
  • Only 50 questions count toward the score; the other 15 are unscored experimental questions
  • You won’t know which questions are scored or not during the test
  • Passing score is approximately 700 out of 1000 (~70%)
  • All questions are multiple choice or multiple response
  • Time allowed: 90 minutes
  • Exam is available in English, Portuguese, Spanish, and other major languages
  • Cost: $100 USD
2. Scoring
  • Only correct answers count toward your score
  • No partial credit on multi-response questions
  • Unanswered questions are marked as incorrect
  • AWS does not reveal the exact weight of each domain
3. Strategy
  • Flag questions to return to them later if unsure
  • Use process of elimination when confused
  • Read all answer choices carefully — some may be similar but only one is correct
  • Practice timing — average ~1.3 minutes per question
  • Focus on understanding concepts, not memorizing definitions
4. Time Management
  • Use the first 60 minutes to go through all questions
  • Leave the last 20–30 minutes to review marked/flagged questions
  • Avoid spending too much time on any single question
